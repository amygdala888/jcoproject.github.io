What is
Jongkong?

Jongkong is a traditional Indonesian dessert from Bangka Belitung, known for its perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors. Made from natural ingredients such as rice flour, coconut milk, and premium palm sugar, it offers a soft and slightly chewy texture.

Wrapped in banana leaves with a fragrant pandan aroma, it delivers a nostalgic and authentic experience that has been cherished for generations.
